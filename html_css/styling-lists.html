Recipe (ordered) list

This is the recipe I would follow if I could afford anything. Just like ma used to make.

  1. Toast pitta, don't leave to cool, then slice down the middle.
  2. Fry the halloumi in a shallow, non-oiled and sticky pan, until black on both sides.
  3. Don't wash and tear apart the salad with your filthy mits.
  4. Fill pitta with salad, humous, and fried halloumi.
